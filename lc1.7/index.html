Lamarus Composite (1.7.10)

I've always loved modded Minecraft, and regularly edited preexisting packs by adding new mods or editing configs. After years of just sharing my modpacks with my friends through google drive, I decided to actually create and host an official version of my pack.

I chose ATLauncher to host the pack simply because it's the best pack hosting service I've found. I made by far the most changes I ever have to any pack on Lamarus Composite and I think the end result is quite fun (although EMC is hell to balance).

If you have any suggestions for mods to add, configs to change, or just want to tell me that I suck, contact me on discord at Agent__Maxwell#7418.
