Guidelines

If none of those services work for you, and you feel that you meet the following criteria, I am happy to help you with what I can.

I would like to mainly focus on people who:

Need help paying for housing, transportation, healthcare, or other critical services.

Need food, medicine, or other critical resources (If food is what you need, I would prefer if you bought Vegan options).

Have debt that they are unable to pay off in a short period.

Are in any other situation where without monetary assistance they will be in danger.


Additionally, if you know anyone who meets these criteria please direct them my way.



Please Do Not Lie

I have no way of vetting you, and I will not ask for personal information or interrogate you (I may ask you some things if your spending seems reckless). This means that you could very easily lie to me. I have no real way to stop you other than asking you not to and explaining why that would be morally bankrupt.

If you take more than you need, someone down the line may not get what they need and could be seriously negatively affected. I do not have infinite resources and will have to readjust how much I can donate depending on how fast they are depleted.



Spend Responsibly

With similar reasoning to the previous paragraph, please spend this money in a frugal manner.

As in:

Buy from thrift stores, Ebay, KSL Classifieds, Facebook Marketplace, BuyNothing.

Shop at Winco or other value-oriented stores (buy the store brand products). However I would prefer if you bought Vegan alternatives even if they are more expensive.

Make use of your local library for books and other media (or pirate).

If I send you money through services like Venmo, do not use the instant transfer option as it has a percentage based fee. If you need something urgently we can work something out.
